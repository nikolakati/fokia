# Source and Rights Memo

## Film

Haxan / Häxan (1922), directed by Benjamin Christensen.

The film is a Swedish-Danish silent horror essay film. Reference sources describe it as a 1922 film written and directed by Christensen, produced by Svensk Filmindustri, and composed of documentary-style material mixed with dramatized scenes about witchcraft, persecution and modern explanations of superstition.

## Rights Reasoning

The film was released in 1922. Many online copies circulate under public-domain claims because of age and historical distribution. However, restorations, new scores, translated intertitles, narration tracks and modern editions may have separate rights.

For this course dataset, the safest approach is to use only a source that explicitly permits access/download or makes a public-domain claim, record the exact URL and version, extract still frames only for educational non-commercial analysis, avoid redistributing a modern restored edition or soundtrack as a film copy, and include this memo with the dataset.

## Version Warning

Haxan exists in multiple versions, including restored versions and the later English-language Witchcraft Through the Ages version with narration. The dataset should identify the exact source version used because frame timestamps, intertitles and runtime may differ.
